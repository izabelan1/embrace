Thanks for visiting Embrace by Salt and Skin

We are Chloe, Tarni and Siobhan, founders of Salt and Skin - beautifully flattering and tonal swimwear and all-natural bodycare products. Our friendship and business was born in Bondi, Australia as after spending ages trying to find swimwear that fit us, we have decided to take matters into our own hands and design something ourselves.

The first designs were a bit wonky but after trial and error we were pleased to launch our first collection.

As we want each of you to feel special, we have decided to launch Embrace, a space for you to inspire and empower one another.

If like us, you were on a journey to uncover your inner confidence and beauty, please share with us your story.

If you would like to visit our shop, please go to the main website.

Our story

  • April 2023

    To celebrate our third birthday, we launched the Embrace project and were invited to speak at the Happy Place Festival at Tatton Park on 2 September 2023.

  • June 2022

    We were featured in the Forbes 30 Under 30 list and opened our first physical store in London.

  • April 2021

    After overcoming lots of obstacles, we created the first line of bikinis which proved to be very popular. Shortly after, we launched our online store.
